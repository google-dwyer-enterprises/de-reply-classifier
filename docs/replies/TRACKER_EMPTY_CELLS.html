The one group worth a closer look: inbound replies

The Last Reply At / Last reply from Instantly columns are blank for 169 of 853 leads. We traced every one:

143
Contacted at their exact address — no reply came back. Blank is correct (booked off-platform, or simply never replied).
14
Address never campaigned in this Instantly workspace — lead came from elsewhere.
9
Same company, different inbox than we emailed. Genuine identity mismatch — but the other inbox has no recoverable reply to attribute.
3
Generic @gmail.com coincidence — not a real same-company match.
Bottom line on inbound blanks: 143 are correctly blank (no reply exists), 14 were never in this workspace, and only ~9 are true identity mismatches — and even those have no reply to recover. This is real-world data reality, not a pipeline gap. (Earlier notes citing ~223 mismatches referred to a different, already-fixed metric: outbound follow-up coverage.)
