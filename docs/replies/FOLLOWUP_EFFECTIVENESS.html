⚠️ Read this before reading the numbers

  1. Descriptive, not causal. These are associations between a follow-up's characteristics and the reply that followed — not proof the characteristic caused the reply.
  2. "Positive" = the next reply was classified booked or interested (within the window before the next follow-up). All other labels were not counted positive.
  3. Warm-lead confound (the big one): 2,804 of these follow-ups went to leads who were already positive before the send (nudges to warm leads). Rather than hide them, "Lead Already Positive" is shown below as its own characteristic — note its very high rate is largely tautological, and treat any body-feature lift that correlates with it with suspicion.
  4. Quoted-thread stripped: 95.7% of bodies were mostly quoted history; features are computed only on the new text Jam wrote (3,562/3,722 cleanly extracted).
  5. Power is limited: 3,722 sends → 443 replies → 335 positive → 117 booked. A value is only shown as a finding with ≥30 sends AND ≥15 positives; everything else is greyed "insufficient data". Booked-only is shown as an overall rate, never sliced.
  6. Client mix confound: positive-rate spans ~7× across clients and one client is ~half the volume. "Largest client" column flags cells dominated by one account — read those as that account's style, not a universal tactic.
  7. Coverage is partial: only one Instantly workspace's outbound is synced; the per-lead backfill is manual; 244 booked leads have zero synced manual follow-ups. This is a labelled snapshot.
  8. AI-judged traits (the "… (AI)" rows): Hook Type, Tone, CTA Style and Personalization are labelled by an AI model (claude-haiku-4-5) reading each message — they are judgement calls on closed categories, not exact facts like length or links. 3,562 follow-ups tagged. Read them as the same kind of association as everything else here.

Timing / survival (NOT a winning characteristic)

Earlier follow-ups show higher positive rates because leads who reply positively early stop getting more follow-ups (the window closes) — this is survival of the unconverted, not proof that later follow-ups are weak.
